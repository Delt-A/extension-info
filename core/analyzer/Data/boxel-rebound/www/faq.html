Q: Why does my app say "Store is not available in your region"?

A: This error is caused by 2 common reasons:

  1. Google does not support Google Pay in your region, or
  2. You are not logged in with your personal Google account.

Q: Why does my app say "Store is not available. Please log in to Google Chrome and turn on sync"?

A: Your browser might have paused sync for whatever reason. Sync is required to auto-backup your extension data and licenses.
